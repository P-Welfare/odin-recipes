P's Famous Cheese Sandwich

Description

Strategic, streamlined, agile and dominant: the modern cheese sandwich is at the cutting edge of the culinary realm.

Hopefully this recipe will allow you to enjoy the highs and lows of competitive sandwiching and give you the edge to dominate your competitors

Ingredients

  • Artisanal Red Leicester Cheese
  • Sourdough bread bought at 5am from the local bakery
  • Farm fresh recently churned butter

Steps

    Spread the butter on the bread while commanding a lackadaisical and nonchalant manner.
    Slice the cheese haphazardly with jaunty movements of the wrist and arm.
    Place the modern cheese Picasso upon its buttery canvas.
    Place the second piece of sourdough bread upon the masterpiece of sliced Red Leicester cheese.
    Press firmly on the top of the bread using the entirety of your bodyweight until the cheese shows through the bread.
    Cut into any shape you like and consume.
