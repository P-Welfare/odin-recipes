P's Famous Egg Salad

Description

I have always been a fan of egg salad as i'm quite fond of the way eggs are shaped. Ever since I was a little boy I enjoyed smashing eggs in the palm of my hand.

I hope this low calorie, high egg, salad recipe is just what you're looking for!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Salad
  • Fork
  • Spoon

Steps

    Cook the egg in the microwave for 30minutes.
    Cut the egg with a sharp knife.
    Take off the egg shell.
    Add egg to salad.
    Add fork or spoon to salad to your taste.oo
